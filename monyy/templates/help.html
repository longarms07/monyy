Confused about how to use Monyy? Fear no more!

This help page will help you better understand how to utilize all of Monyy's features!



Here's an idea of what your page might look like. There's a sidebar and summary sections for "Cash", "Stocks", "Bonds", "Real Estate", and "Debt".


This is an example of the sidebar. You can click on each section header, and it will jump to the part of the web page that holds its summary sections. The same will happen if you click on each individual subsection of the sidebar. You can expand the sidebar to view all subsections and also collapse it again. If that section has a select dropdown menu in the summary section, the summary will reflect whatever subsection of the sidebar you chose.
